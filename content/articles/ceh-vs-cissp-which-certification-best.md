# CEH vs CISSP: Which Cybersecurity Certification Is Better?

CEH and CISSP target different career goals.

## CEH

Best for:

- Penetration Testing
- Ethical Hacking
- Security Assessments

Focuses on offensive security concepts.

## CISSP

Best for:

- Security Leadership
- Architecture
- Governance

Focuses on enterprise security management.

## Which One Should You Choose?

Choose CEH if your goal is technical offensive security.

Choose CISSP if your goal is management, architecture, or leadership.

## Final Thoughts

Neither certification is universally better. The correct choice depends on your desired career path.
