# Google Cybersecurity Certificate Review: Is It Worth It in 2026?

The Google Cybersecurity Certificate remains one of the most popular beginner programs.

## Strengths

- Beginner friendly
- Structured learning path
- Practical exercises
- Industry recognition

## Weaknesses

- Does not replace real-world experience
- Limited depth in advanced topics

## Who Should Take It?

Recommended for:

- Students
- Career changers
- IT beginners

## Final Verdict

The certificate is worth considering if you need a structured introduction to cybersecurity, but it should be combined with projects and hands-on labs.
