# Are Cybersecurity Certifications Enough to Get a Job?

Many beginners believe certifications alone guarantee employment.

## What Employers Want

* Projects
* Labs
* Practical Experience
* Communication Skills

## Common Mistake

Collecting certifications without building practical skills.

## Better Strategy

Combine:

* Certifications
* Projects
* Home Labs
* Portfolio Work

## Final Thoughts

Certifications help open doors, but practical skills usually get candidates hired.
