# How to Become a Penetration Tester in 2026

Penetration testers simulate attacks to identify security weaknesses.

## Step 1

Learn:

- Networking
- Linux
- Windows

## Step 2

Study cybersecurity fundamentals.

Learn:

- Authentication
- Encryption
- Vulnerability Management

## Step 3

Learn common security tools.

Examples:

- Nmap
- Burp Suite
- Wireshark

## Step 4

Practice through labs and CTF competitions.

## Step 5

Build a portfolio and earn relevant certifications.

## Final Thoughts

Practical skills and hands-on experience are more important than collecting certifications alone.
